SPECIAL EVENTS

We would love to host your next event at Munchies! All parties of 15 or more guests are considered special events, requiring a pre-selected, family style, menu as well as a Food and Beverage Minimum.

The Food and Beverage Minimum is the minimum that a party would need to meet, with food and beverages during the course of their events, in the restaurant, in order to reserve the space.

All events are booked for a duration of three hours. We also offer full restaurant buyout options, upon request.
